Our Story

Built for the
AI Economy

Codeline Capital was founded with a singular conviction: that the businesses shaping South Africa's future shouldn't be held back by financing models designed for the past.

Traditional asset finance was built for physical assets — machinery, vehicles, equipment you can see and touch. But the most transformative assets of our era are different. They're AI platforms, autonomous agents, software ecosystems, and intelligent infrastructure that generates returns from day one.

We understand the value of intangible technology assets because we come from the world that builds them. That's why we've created finance structures purpose-built for the AI economy — faster approvals, flexible terms, and a deep understanding of the technology driving your business forward.

Our Vision
To be Africa's leading financier of intelligent technology
We envision a future where every South African business — from ambitious startups to established enterprises — has access to the AI tools they need to compete globally, unconstrained by outdated financing barriers.
Our Mission
Putting businesses first by breaking traditional financing models
We exist to remove the friction between businesses and the AI technologies that will define their future. Through innovative finance structures, rapid decision-making, and a technology-native approach, we empower companies to invest in intelligence — not just assets.
